A Guide To Backyard Dice

What You Need To Play
  • 1 Basket
  • 5 Wooden Dice
  • Backyard Games Scorecard
How To Play
  • All players roll a die with the highest roll starting the game. Play continues clockwise
  • Fill in the top column of the scorecard with players' names
  • For each turn a player has up to three rolls to achieve their desired outcome
  • Roll your dice with the intent of scoring higher than your opponents at the end of the game
  • Each turn and roll you must decide what combo you are after
  • After each turn, the player must fill one entry on their score card
  • Play continues until each player has filled all their boxes for 13 rounds
  • You can fill the boxes in any order that you like but ensure that only one box is filled per round. The only exception is if you roll a Bonus Yankee
First Roll
  • Roll all 5 dice
  • After you have rolled, put aside any dice that you want to keep. If you roll your desired outcome, mark your score and your turn has ended
  • Proceed to your 2nd roll if you want a better combination
Second Roll
  • Roll all dice that you did not set aside
  • If you would like to roll all 5, you can
  • After your roll, put aside any dice that you want to keep. If you roll your desired outcome, mark your score and your turn has ended
  • Proceed to your 3rd roll if you want a better combination
Third Roll (Final Roll)
  • Roll all dice that you did not set aside
  • If you would like to roll all 5, you can
  • You must enter a score at the end of this roll, whether you like your dice combination or not
  • If your dice combination does not fit into any remaining boxes, a zero must be entered into one of your choosing

Top Yahtzee Bonuses!
  • If you roll multiple YAHTZEES, you can rack up a seriously high score.
  • Your first Yahtzee - Score 50 points (Put a 50 in the YAHTZEE bonus box)
  • A further extra bonus Yahtzee:
  • 1. Score 100 points in the appropriate upper section box. For example, if you roll a Yahtzee of 5's, put 25 in the fives box.
  • 2. If the upper box is full, you can put your score in any available lower section box.
  • 3. If both are full, score a Yahtzee with 0 in any of the boxes with your Score Sheet. Score as follows: a.) 50 + Bonus 100 points
Strategy

The player with the highest GRAND TOTAL at the end of the game (i.e. 13 rounds) is the winner. Don't forget to add your GRAND Score! If you score over 63 points in the upper section.

Top Tips & Tactics
  • At the start of your game, you should put aside whatever dice match of the boxes. You must decide which box to fill in that turn.
  • Don't be afraid to score zero in a box if you must.
  • Decide how to score a turn at your roll.
  • Think of scoring between 60-upper section total.
  • The key strategy in the game is not just being a high roller but knowing which box to fill in and when.
