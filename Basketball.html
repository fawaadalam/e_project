üéØ How to Play

1

Start with a jump ball

The game begins with a jump ball at center court, where a referee tosses the ball up between two opposing players.

2

Dribble or pass to move the ball

Players must dribble (bounce) the ball while moving, or pass to teammates to advance the ball up the court.

3

Shoot or drive to score

Players can score by shooting from distance or driving to the basket for a layup or dunk.

4

Defend by blocking shots and stealing

On defense, players work to prevent the opposing team from scoring by blocking shots and stealing the ball.

5

Rebound missed shots

When a shot is missed, players from both teams compete to gain possession of the ball.

6

Win by having the most points at the end!

The team with the highest score when time expires wins the game.
