Volleyball Guide

What is Volleyball?

Volleyball is a fast-paced sport played by two teams, typically consisting of six players each. The game's objective is to score points by sending the ball over the net and into the opponent's court while preventing the opponent from doing the same.

Key Terms in Volleyball

Court

The rectangular playing area, 18 meters long and 9 meters wide, divided by a net.

Net

Divides the court, set at 2.43m for men and 2.24m for women.

Serve

The action of putting the ball into play from behind the backline.

Spike

An aggressive attack hit, sending the ball forcefully over the net.

Basic Rules and Instructions

  • Each team can touch the ball up to three times before sending it over the net.
  • Points are scored on every serve (rally scoring).
  • A set is won by the first team to reach 25 points (with a two-point lead).
  • Players must rotate positions clockwise after winning a serve.

Volleyball Etiquette

Respect

Show respect to teammates, opponents, and referees.

Communication

Maintain clear communication with your team.

Fair Play

Adhere to the rules and accept referee decisions.

Warm-Up

Always warm up properly to prevent injuries.
